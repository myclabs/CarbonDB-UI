What's new

Here is a log of main changes on functionalities and data. For a fully detailed log, please consult the data and inference engine open source projects on Github (user interface application project is not open source at this stage).

  • 2014-10-28 [Functionalities] In the graph view arrows are now coloured, depending on the nature of the corresponding relation.
  • 2014-10-28 [Data] Primary energy extraction data added for upstream of fossil fuels (and as a consequence for any downstream process). You can now view the amount of primary energy (either the total or only under the form of say crude oil) extracted in order to produce 1 kg of yoghurt!
  • 2014-10-28 [Data] Refactoring of upstream and combustion of fuels. The model for upstream of fuel now takes into account various steps (production and conditioning at source, transportation to market, refining near market). Most of source data are now referenced. Much still remains to be done (fuel distribution, upstream of solid fossil fuels, etc). Some issues have been created on Github.
  • 2014-10-21 [Functionalities] List of references.
  • 2014-10-15 [Functionalities] Members of a dimension can be ordered at hand (they are not necessarily displayed in alphabetical order).
  • 2014-10-15 [Functionalities] Tree and global graph on separate pages.
  • 2014-10-13 [Functionalities] Display of references.
  • 2014-10-09 [Functionalities] Addition of single process and single coefficient views.
  • 2014-10-09 [Functionalities] Display of comments on groups. At this stage few comments exist among the data, but you can find one for group "Mass of agricultural input used for cultivation of a unit of area".
  • 2014-10-09 [Functionalities] Addition of pages "What's new" and "Known bugs".
  • 2014-10-07 [Functionalities] In the view on a group of processes, display of values ands units for various impacts and elementary flows (instead of the single "100 years global warming potential GHG" impact that was displayed up to now).
  • 2014-09-30 [Data] Addition of air freight transport group and jet fuel combustion and upstream.
  • 2014-08-26 Online application launched! Embryonic data (categories, groups, relations, in order to display functionalities). Tree of categories and graph of groups of processes available on home page. View of a group of processes displays the the single "100 years global warming potential GHG" impact and upstream and downstream relations.
